Drop and Give Me Fitness Anywhere

Drop and Give Me combines fun and fitness into one app. Compete against yourself and your friends in fitness challenges. Train for strength with bodyweight exercise progressions. Share your results on Facebook. See how you measure up when you Drop and Give Me!

  • Fitness ChallengesBecome a fitness machine with short challenges that can be completed anywhere using only your body weight, then dare your friends to beat your score.
  • Strength TrainingEmerge from our six-week training program as a stronger, faster, leaner version of you. Perfect for getting ready for that big event.

  • The Team

  • Fitness Challenges

    Five levels of increasingly difficult challenges designed to test your current fitness level. Record your high score and NO CHEATING. Don't worry. You can go back and get a better score once we've trained you to be a muscle-bound strong man competitor.

  • Strength Training

    Six-week long training program of increasingly difficult movements designed to take only 10-15 minutes three times week. Become impossibly fast and strong! Outrun your friends! Show off bigger pecs than a football player! Hear the Rocky theme song (in your head) as you work out!

  • Activity Log

    Record everything you have done in your quest to be the best. Keep track of all the training you have done, all the challenges you have won (and lost), all the times you cheated and we caught you (Santa sees all). Also holds the What's Next box, just in case you were wondering what kind of torture you get to play with tomorrow.

  • Share with your Friends

    We know you're competitive. We know you like to torture your friends. Let us help you with that. Boast to your friends how awesome you're doing at the challenges and make them get you back by beating your score.

    Warning: We take no responsibility for your friends beating your score. It just means you have to train harder, lazybones.

The Team

Drop and Give Me started as Matt and Daniel's final project for the Mobile Application Development (CS 4520) course at Northeastern University. Based on feedback from classmates and friends, Matt and Daniel decided to publicly release Drop and Give Me on the Android market.
